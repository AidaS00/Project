About the shelter

The history of "Cat&Dog" began in 2010, when a temporary shelter was founded for 200 dogs rescued from trapping services. Today the shelter is the only humane solution to the social problem of homeless animals in our city. The purpose of creating the shelter was to provide assistance to homeless and abandoned animals. Later, on the basis of the shelter, the Animal Security public fund was created, one of the most important tasks of which is to foster a humane attitude towards animals in society. The shelter has already acquired its own territory - thanks to the help of volunteers, a land plot of 5.3 hectares has been purchased. Currently, there are about 150 dogs on the territory of the shelter. The shelter is funded by charitable donations.
